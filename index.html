About me

Hi! I'm Jamie.

From helping you know what you want, to unlocking the newest technologies, to fine tuning your online store -- I can create all aspects of your project. I have over 2 years’ experience building webpages, on-time and within the budget. Contact me for an assessment meeting so we build this now.

I'm a freelance, front-end developer currently completing a master's in web development at The Open University of Catalonia. I believe that good code involves names that reveal intent, standard conventions, functions that do one thing only, comments that warn about consequences, readable tests, and no duplications.

Along with being highly skilled with HTML, CSS and JavaScript, I am especially interested in building with React.

I excel at good UI/UX design which reduces frustration by "not making the user think", creating clear navigation, and allowing a clear way back. I am focused on creating usability by getting directly to the point and using recognizable web forms where expected.
